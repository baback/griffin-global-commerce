Our Services

Everything you need to go from concept to global market.

We offer three core service areas that work together or independently, depending on where you are in your journey.

Crowdfunding

We manage end-to-end crowdfunding campaigns on Kickstarter and Indiegogo. From pre-launch audience building to post-campaign fulfillment, we handle the strategy and execution so you can focus on your product.

  • Campaign strategy & page design
  • Video direction & creative assets
  • Paid media & PR outreach
  • Backer engagement & fulfillment
Learn more about crowdfunding

E-Commerce

We build and optimize online stores that convert. Whether you're transitioning from a crowdfunding campaign or starting fresh, we set up multi-platform storefronts that are ready to sell.

  • Shopify & Amazon store setup
  • Brand identity & product photography
  • Conversion rate optimization
  • Multi-channel sales strategy

International Trade

Ready to sell beyond your home market? We handle the complexity of international expansion — logistics, compliance, and distribution — so you can focus on growing your brand.

  • Global supply chain management
  • Customs & regulatory compliance
  • Multi-market distribution
  • International fulfillment & 3PL

Not sure where to start?

We'll help you figure out the right approach for your product and stage. No commitment required.
